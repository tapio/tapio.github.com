Roguelikes

I've always been interested in procedural generation, so roguelikes have been a natural fit for me. The games below are small but complete - most are done as entries to the annual Seven Day Roguelike Challenge.

  • 7DRL 2019: Pour Me More [dev blog]
    • Overcooked-inspired mobile friendly bartender roguelike-like with co-op multiplayer.
  • 7DRL 2016: Fantastic Dungeons [dev blog]
    • Mobile friendly graphical roguelike where you try to reach the end of the dungeon levels.
  • 7DRL 2015: Beware the Space Bears [dev blog]
    • Mobile friendly scifi themed survival roguelike
  • 7DRL 2014: Golem Quest [dev blog]
    • Kill monsters and drink potions in glorious 3D
  • 7DRL 2013: Lawman [dev blog]
    • My first and so far only failure to complete a 7DRL; would have been a westerner
  • 7DRL 2012: Infiniverse [dev blog]
    • Ambitious space exploration
  • 7DRL 2011: A Little Anxious When It's Dark [dev blog]
    • Top-down 3d WebGL dungeon hack
  • 7DRL 2010: City of the Condemned (pictured) [dev blog]
    • Angels vs. demons in battle for human souls
  • 1kbrl - A tiny roguelike in C++ and FreeBASIC

Also related:

  • Unicodetiles.js - A JavaScript character based tile engine.
